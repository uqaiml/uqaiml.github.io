About Us

UQ Artifical Intelligence and Machine Learning Club (UQ AIML) focuses on data science, machine learning and artifical intelligence. UQ AIML, formerly known as UQ Statistics Society, has existed as part of UQ’s Student Association of Science Societies (SASS) for a number of years. This year we aim to replicate the success in these areas and build on the society’s reach and influence. We will bring a renewed focus on industry networking, and expand the scope of our activities to modern applications, such as data science and machine learning.

AIML x MSS: Maths Behind AI

Talk by Prof. Drik Kroese in collaboration with UQ Mathematics Students Society

AIML x MSS: Maths Behind AI is an educational event that explores the mathematical concepts behind artificial intelligence and machine learning. The program aims to demystify the complex algorithms used in AI and make them more accessible to a wider audience. Participants will learn about key concepts such as linear algebra, calculus, and probability theory, and how they are used to create intelligent machines that can learn and make decisions.
